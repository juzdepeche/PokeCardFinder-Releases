# PokeCardFinder

Find the best prices for your Pokémon TCG cards across Canadian stores.

Paste your decklist, hit Search, and PokeCardFinder scrapes 10+ Canadian stores in parallel and tells you exactly where to buy each card and from how many stores to minimize your total cost.

---

## Features

- **Multi-store search** - scrapes 10+ Canadian stores simultaneously
- **Best price per card** - finds the best condition and price, English first with French fallback
- **Reprint detection** - suggests valid reprints when an exact set match isn't available
- **Shopping optimizer** - recommends a plan that balances card cost vs. number of stores (accounts for shipping)
- **Card images** - thumbnails and full-size images for every card in your list
- **Market price comparison** - see how store prices stack up against TCGPlayer market value
- **Cart** - track what you've decided to buy across sessions
- **Export** - copy your shopping list per store to clipboard or CSV
- **Dark mode**

## Installation

1. Download the latest installer from the [Releases](https://github.com/juzdepeche/PokeCardFinder-releases/releases/latest) page
2. Run `PokeCardFinder Setup x.x.x.exe`
3. Enter your license key to activate

> **Windows only.** Your saved data, cart, and settings are preserved across updates.
